The best dupes aren't always found in specialist shops. Zara is proof of that. Between €10 and €30, the brand offers fragrances openly inspired by perfumery classics — never exact copies, but often close enough to matter.

Here's our honest take.

---

## What Zara Does Differently

Most dupe brands stay quiet about their inspirations. Zara plays a different game: collaborations with renowned perfumers, seriously built formulas, and global distribution that keeps prices accessible.

This isn't supermarket fragrance. It's accessible perfumery — with real qualities, and clear limits.

---

## The Weaknesses You Should Know About

Let's be upfront about what doesn't work.

**Longevity is the weak spot.** Many Zara references are light eau de toilette formulations. They fade within a few hours, especially on dry skin. If you're looking for a fragrance that lasts a full day, some references will disappoint you.

**Complexity is limited.** Note transitions — those subtle evolutions from morning to afternoon — are often fast or absent. What you smell on the first spray is largely what you'll have all day.

**Stock issues.** The best references sell out fast. When a Zara fragrance blows up, it disappears from shelves within weeks. Collections rotate regularly, without always being restocked.

---

## How to Get the Most Out of Them

Zara fragrances reward a careful application:

- **Moisturised skin.** Dry skin absorbs fragrance faster. Applying after a shower on slightly damp skin — or over a neutral moisturiser — makes a real difference to longevity.
- **Heat points.** Neck, wrists, inner elbow — areas where blood flows close to the surface diffuse better.
- **Layering.** Several Zara fragrances work well together. It's an easy way to personalise your scent and extend the sillage.

---

## Our Honest Verdict

Zara isn't replacing your niche fragrances. Nobody's claiming otherwise.

But for rotating regularly, exploring new scent profiles, or building a varied fragrance wardrobe without spending a fortune — it's one of the best options on the market at this price point. Some references are genuinely good. Others are forgettable.

The key is knowing which ones to pick. That's exactly what we're here for.
